Towers of Hanoi

Objective

Move all of the rings from the left tower to the right tower, in a stack from smallest on top to largest on bottom. Try to do so in as few moves as possible.

Instructions

  • Use the ← and → keys to select a ring or tower
  • Use the ↑ key to remove a ring and the ↓ key to place a ring onto a tower
  • Note: only one ring can be moved at a time (the top ring on any tower)
